Iwanai Area Ship Lift Corporation

Professional Boat Haul-Out & Storage Facility at Iwanai Port, Hokkaido

Supporting Safe Vessel Maintenance at Iwanai Port

Iwanai Area Ship Lift Corporation operates a boat haul-out facility located at Iwanai Port in Hokkaido, Japan.

We provide safe and efficient haul-out and launching services for commercial fishing vessels and recreational boats, supporting inspections, maintenance, repairs, and seasonal storage.

Lift-Type Haul-Out Facility

Our facility uses a lift-type haul-out system designed to raise and launch vessels safely and efficiently while reducing stress on the hull.

The facility can be used for hull cleaning, bottom inspections, maintenance work, and other vessel care needs.

For Pleasure Boats

Pleasure boat use is available under an annual contract system. Dry storage and day-use options are available depending on the type of use.

For Commercial Fishing Vessels

Our facility is available for fishing vessels requiring haul-out, launching, inspections, maintenance, and repair work. We welcome vessels from Iwanai Port and neighboring ports.

Our Services

Our facility supports haul-out and launching, hull cleaning, bottom inspections, winter storage, and dry storage for pleasure boats.

Enjoy the Beauty of Iwanai

Located on the Sea of Japan, Iwanai Port is a center for commercial fishing, marine recreation, and tourism, offering beautiful coastal scenery throughout the year.

About Iwanai Port

Iwanai Port is located on the western coast of Hokkaido along the Sea of Japan.

The port serves both the local fishing industry and recreational boating community while providing easy access to the surrounding coastline.

Our haul-out facility is conveniently located within the port, offering an ideal environment for vessel maintenance and storage.
